The Story

I didn't plan on becoming a brand. I was just being me. Saying the things everyone else was thinking but afraid to say out loud.

Turns out, a lot of people needed that. So here we are.

Delusional Dumb Grandpa is more than a name. It's a mindset. It's about living life your way, laughing loud, and not apologizing for it.
